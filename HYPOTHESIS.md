MOVE 2 HYPOTHESIS — written before any code

The claim:
A cohort learner who can define API or backend will still fail to 
derive *why* the abstraction exists from first principles when pushed 
with one sharp follow-up — in at least 2 out of 3 sessions.

Result that proves me right:
At least 2 out of 3 cold users hit a genuine gap (fall back to 
analogy, need a hint, or go silent) under one sharp follow-up.

Result that means no problem exists:
All 3 cold users derive cleanly with no hints needed.

Kill-number:
If 0 out of 2 cold users fail under a follow-up, my hypothesis 
was wrong and this tool finds nothing real.
